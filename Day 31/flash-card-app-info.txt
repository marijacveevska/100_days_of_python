The flash card app is made for me to practice my Dutch language.
To reach an A2 (elementary) level in Dutch, you generally need to learn between 1,000 and 2,500 words.
How this app works is the following:
1. Dutch word appears on the screen and after 5 second the english word is shown.
If you don't know the word you click x and that word is added in the words to learn file that you can review in order to learn the words.
If you know the word you click ✔ and it will just continue with the next chosen random word. 
    (if that word was previously in the words to learn file it will now be removed since you said you know the word)
